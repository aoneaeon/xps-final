Frequently Asked Questions

What makes this property stand out?

It's a 4-hectare property subdivided into small cuts, with almost every lot possessing a stunning sea-view and mountain-view at nearly 360 degrees...and it's dirt cheap. ;-)

What are the typical lot sizes?

Lot sizes vary to cater to different needs and preferences, but they range from approximately 753 sqm to over 1,800 sqm. You can view specific details for each available lot on the Available Lots page or by hovering over the Interactive Map above.

What is the condition of the roads?

The roads are 5 meters wide and include a base course.

What are my payment options?

You get a 10% discount off the total price if you pay the full amount upfront (in cash or a single payment). For installment, there is a 30% down payment, and the 70% balance is spread out over a 12-month payment plan...with 0% interest!

How much is the reservation fee? Is it refundable?

The reservation fee is a non-refundable ₱20,000, but is deductible from the last payment.

How close is it to the beachside?

300 meters away, a 5-minute drive.
